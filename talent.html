.VESNA

.VESNA is a groundbreaking artist that takes the audience to an unforgettable journey through the world of deep, organic and progressive house. .VESNA’s exquisite sets are defined by a tasty blend of velvet melodies layered on top of moving deep house vibes and silky-smooth baselines. Meticulously selected tracks come together into soul-touching music creations that make you dance from dusk to dawn. A long-time Burning Man veteran, performer, and ambassador, he adds the sacred desert vibe into many of his amazing sets. Check out SoundCloud links to the right for .VESNA’s latest music journeys.

.STARDUST

.STARDUST is a flood of sunshine, good vibes, and joy with the amazing healing power against bad mood, grinding stress and dull weekends. His energizing and uplifting sets blend nu disco, organic house and dance pop into a tasty cocktail of emotions and positive energy. His music makes you dance, smile and open up. Check out .STARDUST’s latest sets on SoundCloud and stay tuned for new ones soon!
